1.  **$\mathcal{V}_{cl}$ (Classical Volume Modulus):**

    $$ \delta_{Casimir} = \frac{1}{24 \cdot \mathcal{V}_{cl}} $$

3.  **$\delta_{Instanton}$ (Genus-1 Correction):**
